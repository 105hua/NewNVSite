So, what is Near Vanilla ?

We promote a low stress community atmosphere, where we can take our time, think before we act and have fun together! We respect each other and our differences. We act and communicate responsibly and take responsibility for our actions in game. It is a server free from griefing and thieving, and we have moderators to ensure that. We aim for a simple yet enjoyable server. If there are suggestions made by any player, we will take a look at them and maybe they might be implemented!

Our Amazing Community ...

Near Vanilla has an extremely diverse and friendly community that is always willing to help out. Whether you need a few items or some help with that pesky mod that refuses to work, we cannot wait for you to join us!

Check out our Server Specs ...

When it comes to running a Minecraft server, we understand that performance is a top priority. Near Vanilla is hosted using top of the line hardware in order to provide our community with a seamless experience!

Processor

An Octa-Core Processor, capable of reaching speeds of 5GHz provides you with seamless gameplay on NearVanilla. Having a processor that is easily able to handle the load of our Minecraft server is crucial to keeping lag spikes and rubberbanding to a minimum.

Storage

Our server is prepared for all scenarios, with 1TB of SSD Storage. This allows us to store bi-hourly backups of the server, ensuring that lost or corrupted data can be restored quickly and efficiently, while also minimizing the effect it may have on our community.

RAM

With 8GB of Dedicated RAM allocated to our server, players need not worry about random server crashes that could result in a loss of data. Our players are able to enjoy a hassle-free experience, with the peace of mind that their progress is safe and secure.

Network

When running a multiplayer server, we understand that a stable network connection is crucial. Our server is hosted on a 1Gbps connection, ensuring that players experience minimal latency and are able to enjoy a lag-free experience on NearVanilla.

Take a look at some of the Plugins we use...

DeathLog

Can't remember where you died? No problem! DeathLog keeps track of where you died without the requirement of the F3 Screen!

Dynmap

A Google Maps like map viewer for our server that is viewable within your browser!

CapAFK

A simple yet crucial plugin that kicks AFK players that do not complete a Captcha.

CoreProtect

A fast, efficient and detailed data logging and anti-griefing tool, allowing us to keep track of all player actions and revert any wrongdoing.

DiscordSRV

A powerful, highly-configurable, open-source Discord to Minecraft Bridging Plugin. This allows you to communicate with players on the server from Discord, and vice versa!

One Player Sleep

A simple plugin that allows a single player to fast-forward through the night, without the need for all players to sleep! Want to keep the night? No problem, just wake the person sleeping up, though we can't guarantee they'll be happy about it!

IsSlimeChunk

Another simple plugin that allows players to easily check if they are standing in a Slime Chunk!

LuckPerms

A powerful permissions plugin that ensures that players have the correct permissions to perform the actions they need to, while also ensuring that they do not have access to commands they shouldn't.
